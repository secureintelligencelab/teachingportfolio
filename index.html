Teaching Philosophy & Approach

Core Philosophy: Education should be broad, practical, and inclusive, equipping students not only with theoretical knowledge but also with skills that prepare them for professional success. I aim to cover a comprehensive range of topics within the subject domain while ensuring that the content is communicated in a clear and accessible way.

Real-World Application Focus

I emphasize teaching that connects directly to real-world applications, enabling students to see how concepts translate into practice. This includes integrating materials and exercises that prepare students for:

  • Job interview preparation
  • Professional certifications
  • Industry career guidance

Equity & Inclusiveness

Equity and inclusiveness are central to my approach. I focus on each student's learning needs, providing equal attention regardless of gender, ethnicity, or background. I make it a priority to respond to student queries promptly and offer solutions that are both supportive and consistent with CIHE policies.

Lifelong Learning Motivation

I seek to motivate students to engage in self-directed exploration of emerging industry trends, tools, and certifications, fostering lifelong learning habits. My teaching is not just about delivering content but about inspiring curiosity, confidence, and professional growth in every student.

Demonstrated Excellence in Teaching

Interactive Learning Implementation

I introduced interactive lecture notes that combine topic discussions with activities such as short answers, matching, ordering, debates, and MCQs.

Example: Introduction to Information Security - Interactive Notes

These replace traditional lecture slides to create more engaging classroom experiences.

Structured Assessment System

In cybersecurity classes, students:

  • Generate a PDF in the first hour
  • Engage in practical tasks throughout the session
  • Complete open-book quizzes in the final half-hour
  • Submit 5 hands-on tests on Moodle per unit

Across 10 weeks, quizzes contribute 10% of overall grade, ensuring consistent participation and focus.
